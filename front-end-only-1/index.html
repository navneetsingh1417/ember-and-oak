Our Story

Born from a love of fire,
craft and honest cooking

Ember & Oak began as a simple idea: that the oldest cooking techniques, when combined with exceptional ingredients and quiet obsession, can create something truly memorable. Our wood-fired kitchen is the heartbeat of everything we do.

Every dish is built around what is at its very best — from coastal fishermen and family farms to foragers who walk the same hills season after season. We believe cooking should honour the ingredient, not overshadow it.

"The wood fire is not theatre — it is the method. Everything we cook, we cook with intention."

— James Whitmore, Head Chef & Co-Founder
